About us

Dispose and earn is an online company that deals with second hand items that are to be disposed by people
but instead can be used for other uses or even sold to other people who are in need.It also creates a platform
that links people and companies for recycling of products considered as waste but can be reused as raw materials
to make other products in the industry.The main aim for the firm is to reduce environmental pollution
and wastage of products that can be used to make other by-poducts.It is also created to foster employment
opportunities for the youths in the communities as they may be employed in the packaging and transportation sector.

For security purposes and comfortable transactions we have partnered with
afaricom M-pesa and Glovo to ensure transactions have trails
until delivered to your door step with our team.
